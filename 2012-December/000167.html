Hi Arthur,

I now managed to establish a connection with the device. The error had nothing to do with CoE, but rather with an incomplete/incorrect configuration of DC. The other EtherCAT slaves we used so far simply did not care about DC, but this device obviously does. With correct DC, ec_receive_processdata() works as intended.
