No clue what is wrong with your set up. Most people just simply download 
and build without issues.
I had a look at one of my PC's, running Ubuntu 12 64bit.

The call chain for socket.h is:
/usr/include/x68_64-linux-gnu/sys/socket.h
/usr/include/x68_64-linux-gnu/bits/socket.h
/usr/include/x68_64-linux-gnu/asm/socket.h
/usr/include/asm-generic/socket.h

The last file has the actual definitions your set-up complains about.

build-essential should do the trick, but you can also reinstall glibc6 
and glibc6-dev.
