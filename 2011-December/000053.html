I have a small patch for SOEM (available at 
http://git.mech.kuleuven.be/?p=robotics/soem.git;a=blob;f=soem_core/rtnet.patch;h=ba80c8118a75cf12e9afabf3c57faec851054f90;hb=refs/heads/master)
that enables the use of SOEM on top of RTNET which in itself can be used on 
top of Xenomai( or RTAI-lxrt).

This patch is well tested and I have confirmed that no mode (hard-soft-hart rt)  
switches happen during send_process_data - receive_process_data cycli on a 
Xenomai kernel.
