Hello.

I found simple bug in linux version of function ecx_setupnic in file nicdrv.c. There are used "logical or" operators instead of "bitwise or" operators.
Wrong line:
   ifr.ifr_flags = ifr.ifr_flags || IFF_PROMISC || IFF_BROADCAST;
Should be:
   ifr.ifr_flags = ifr.ifr_flags | IFF_PROMISC | IFF_BROADCAST;
