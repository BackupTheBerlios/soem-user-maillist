>comparing to the wireshark capture from TwinCAT, I've noticed that my sdo is a byte longer
SOEM sends a "normal" SDO transfer. TwinCAT uses an "expedited" transfer. If you dig deeper into the wireshark trace you will notice the subtle differences.

Anyway it seems we have another Beckhoff slave that will fail the
    slave conformance test. All slaves should accept both expedited and
    normal transfers. The work around is to make a special SDOwrite with
    expedited transfer type. The new to be released SOEM has expedited
    SDO functions (but for other reasons).
