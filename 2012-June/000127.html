Hi All,

using the ethercat master to configure several different types of devices
(expecially servo-drives) i found out some quite big differences in the
CANopen objects mapped in every single slave, even regarding objects
involved in the configuration phase (like 1C00 or 1C32 and 1C33).

For example I found out that some salves (with both inputs and outputs) do
not have the objects 1C32 and 1C33 mapped even if they support DC Sync. 

I also see that the ethercat master rely on these objects, for example, to
configure the DC Sync.

I have searched into all the ETHERCAT documentation to find out if, for
example, the objects 1C32 and 1C33 are mandatory but I found different
answers.for example in the document "ETG1000_6_S_R_V1i0i2_EcatALProtocols"
these objects are marked as OPTIONAL but in the document
"ETG5001_1_V0i1i1_S_D_MDP_GeneralSpec" they are marked as CONDITIONAL
(MANDATORY if slave has outputs and/or inputs thus for a servo drive
MANDATORY) but this document is about "modular device".

My question is: are these objects really CONDITIONAL or are they OPTIONAL??
